I finished my undergraduate studies in Computer Engineering from Ganpat University in 2016. Since then, I have been working as a software engineer, contributing to the design and development of Business applications and Enterprise products. My professional career began with Tata Consultancy Services, where I worked as a full stack developer. Later in 2019, I joined QMetry, as a member of the backend development team for analytics and reporting.

In late 2020, I moved to the United States for pursuing Master's in Computer Science from Northeastern University. My graduate studies are focussed on learning programming design patterns and techniques for large scale parallel data processing.

When I'm not coding, you will either find me in the kitchen trying to cook something or biking around the city observing life. Also, I love taking photos, some of which you can find here.

I recently completed my summer internship in data engineering at Amazon. I am now back to school and will be looking to full-time roles starting in late 2022.
